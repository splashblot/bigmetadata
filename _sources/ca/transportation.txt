Median commuting duration (female)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------











Measure "Median commuting duration (female)"  for one point:


.. code-block:: postgresql





  UPDATE {table_name}
    SET {new_numeric_column} =
      OBS_GetMeasure(
        CDB_LatLng(43.6559499126, -79.3796539307),
        'ca.statcan.cols_nhs.t017c002_f'
      );






Median commuting duration (female) is only available for point lookups.

.. code-block:: postgresql





  UPDATE {table_name}
    SET {new_numeric_column} =
      OBS_GetMeasure(
        CDB_LatLng(43.6559499126, -79.3796539307),
        'ca.statcan.cols_nhs.t017c001_f'
      );





Measure "Total employed population aged 15 years and over with a usual place of work or no fixed workplace address by median commuting duration (female)" within an area:

          ST_Buffer(CDB_LatLng(43.6559499126, -79.3796539307), 0.01),
