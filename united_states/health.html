11.7.1. Female life expectancy¶

UPDATE {table_name}
  SET {new_numeric_column} =
    OBS_GetMeasure(
      CDB_LatLng(40.7, -73.9),
      'us.ihme.female_life_expectancy'
    );

Female life expectancy is only available for point lookups.
